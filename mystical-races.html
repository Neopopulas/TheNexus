Notable Natural Locations Rules

Overview

Kith has, for as long as anyone can remember, been the home to humans and humans alone. Despite this - and despite the only minimal evidence - many people believe that their used to be other races. Evidence of their races can be seen in lost ruins, unknown carvings and ancient tales of strange people from the lands around Kith. Regardless of what one may or may not believe, remember that for the vast majority of people these are little more than fairy tales.

Elves

Elves are said to be tall and slender beings, sometimes reaching seven feet tall or more. They are said to have fair skin and eyes like jewels and hair the colour of the sun and the stars. They are said to live impossibly long lives and to have knowledge and power beyond that of humans. It is said that they had existed for thousands of years before humans were even a thought and that had not some calamity befallen them they would have ruled the world until the end of time.

Description

The stories of elves are often fanciful and fantastical but also tend to be strangely specific when it comes to their appearance. Some scholars who make this their expertise - and usually lose the respect of their peers in the process - have reportedly found detailed descriptions in old carvings found in the Eastern edges of Kith, especially along the Thorn Wallwhen it is cleared enough to reveal some of the lost buildings there. Elves are described - apparently - as between six and a half and seven and a half feet tall. Slender of build but surprisingly strong, they are said to have pale skin and ‘jewel coloured eyes’ - it is assumed colours of green and blue but some say it would make sense for other colours to exist such as gold, red and violet and hair the colour of the sun and stars, implying colours of red, gold and white. It is said that they had the normal five digits but each of their fingers had not the normal four joints but five, making their fingers exceptionally long. Descriptions say that they tended to not wear armour of any kind but carried long, serpentine curved swords.

Region

The only region where any evidence - if it can be called that - of elves is said to be found in the east, in the often abandoned villages on the far east, and along the path of the Thorn Wall, most of the time these sites are overrun by the growth of the wall but when the Knights of the Thorn have passed by and beaten back the wall sometimes entire settlements have been uncovered, for a short time.

Dwarves

Little is assumed about Dwarves as they left no ruins within the Kingdom. They are said to have lived in the northern mountains - known as the Crown of Bones Mountains- and while there is some minor evidence of this, the proof of them being little more than stories is minimal at best. They are said to have been crafters of create skill and have had access to a great deal of technological power. There is a persistent myth that many of the core technology that the kingdom makes use of - such as metal and stone working - came from some bygone trade with these creatures.

Description

Dwarves are said to be short and stocky, much shorter than the humans and are said to maybe even be furred. Stories say that these creatures were extremely strong despite their size and they feared open water as a mortal threat. They are said to have only two hair colours, coal black and steel grey.

Region

Dwarves are only known to have existed in the North, most likely far up in the mountains, where no one has been in generations. There is no evidence that the dwarves ever had any presence in the Kingdom itself.

Orcs

Orcs are not entirely a myth, and are considered to be a true threat. While they are rare to see, there is hard evidence that they do exist in the mountains and will very occasionally venture south far enough to enter the Kingdom and harry the settlers and minors in the north of the Kingdom, most prominently in the Whitecrest Duchy, where they are a great enough threat that there are often dedicated soldiers stationed in vulnerably outposts. They are a brutal and sadistic race that seems to raid not for plunder or land, but simply to kill anything and everything they can, be it livestock or humans, it has been suggested that they routinely venture south to prevent - or discourage - humans from venturing further north into the mountains.

Description

Orcs are large and imposing figures their skin ranges between grey and onyx, many orcs don’t have hair at all but those that do tend to have grey or black hair. Most orcs have yellow or red eyes. Orcs tend to stand at least six feet tall, but usually much larger, and are heavily muscled. They seem to have no access to refined clothing or weapons, most of what they wear and carry seem to either be crudely crafted themselves or scavenged during their raids.


Region

Orcs exist - as far as is known - only in the north. Primarily in the Crown of Bones Mountains. There have been no excursions far enough north to be sure that the orcs exist only in the mountains, or whether they cross over from the other side. There has never been any evidence of an orc kingdom or even an outpost in any of the mountain areas that the Kingdom has explored. It is possible that the orcs are often driven south from the mountains by another, greater external threat, but their lack of desire to claim any land that they raid seems to go against this. Another theory states that perhaps orcs live only in the mountains and are driven south into the Kingdom by a larger, more deadly external threat.

Fey

The fey have been in stories of the kingdom for generation. They are said to steal children, intermix with humans without their knowledge for reasons unknown and seem to become attached to certain families and stick with them for generations. Their abilities are unknown, and whether they actually exist at all is questionable, if they do they have vast powers and an unknowable plan that makes them extremely dangerous.

Desription

The Fey are said to be beautiful and ethereal, but that is about all the description that seems to agree. Some stories claim they possess animal traits like horns and hooves and wings, while others say they are more like ghosts, formless and wraith-like. Many of the duchies have their own unique concept of what the Fey may look like, but the Summerset Duchy seems to have the largest number of myths surrounding them.

Giants

Like the Orcs, giants are said to exist in the north. Unlike the orcs, there is little actual evidence of them, no one has been able to provide any proof that they exist, or that anyone has actually fought one. They are said to perhaps be the masters of the orcs, or simply live in the same areas and due to their large size and powerful nature they do not fear the orcs.

Desription

Giants are said to be at least fifteen feet tall and seem to be as much made from stone as they are from flesh. They entirely lack hair and seem impervious to the cold, wearing little in the way of clothing and nothing in the way of armour. They are said to weird huge stone pillars, or enormous wooden clubs when in combat.

Region

Like the orcs, Giants are said to only exist in the northern mountains. There is no known settlement of giants.
